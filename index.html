Cookie Tools

Since June 1st 2015 EU applies the so called "cookie law". This webpage is not showing a cookie disclaimer and will not show one anytime soon, because it does not use them.

A few words on the law

This page wants to help you understand what you need to do and what not, as well as displaying tools for the most common platforms to implement that law.

This webpage is in no way in favor of the cookie law, and is in fact deeply against that law. A social issue can and will not be solved by impeding a specific technical solution. New solutions will come and they will be just more complicated to avoid and to legalize. What is the next step? Illegality of <frame> and <iframe> elements? No more <embed>? No more javascript? Sorry, but that is not the Internet made of hypertexts that it could and should be, it's a regression. Privacy is a concern? It sure has to be, but saying that a "cookie awareness program" solves the privacy issues is just like saying that knowing the calories on a cookie will stop you from eating it. It's at best hypocritical, if not simply a justification of public money for a stupid and useless law.

Yet, at the moment we have to comply.
